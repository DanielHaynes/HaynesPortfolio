About

Hi, my name is Daniel Haynes and I am a self taught Software Engineer with 5 years of industry experience ranging from Front-End Developement to developing 2D and 3D games in Unity. I started my career in Quality Assurance and quickly found a passion for programming. By teaching myself programming during nights and weekends I was able to sieze an opportunity at Age of Learning, Inc and work my way into a Front-End Developer position. Since then I have been actively pursuing my Bachlors of Science in Computer Science.
